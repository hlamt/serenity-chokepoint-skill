# Anti-Patterns

Avoid outputs that:

1. Generate stock trade instructions.
2. Make return promises.
3. Treat a temporary shortage as a structural chokepoint without testing persistence.
4. Treat a popular narrative as an investment opportunity without a value-chain mechanism.
5. Directly equate Chokepoint Theory with TOC / Theory of Constraints.
6. Give deterministic conclusions when evidence is missing, stale, or single-sourced.
7. Omit invalidation conditions.
8. List companies without explaining exposure, value capture, valuation risk, and evidence gaps.
9. Use “瓶颈” without specifying supply-chain chokepoint, local bottleneck, or TOC-style constraint.
10. Hide counterevidence or constraint-migration risk.
