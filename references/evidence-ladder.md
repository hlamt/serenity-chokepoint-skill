# Evidence Ladder

Use the highest level supported by the full evidence set, not by its strongest isolated item.

| Level | Definition | Typical handling |
| --- | --- | --- |
| L0 | 观点 / 叙事 | Treat as a question generator only. |
| L1 | 公开新闻 | Confirm date, original source, and whether claims are independently reported. |
| L2 | 公司公告 / 财报 / 招股书 | Prefer exact document sections; note issuer incentives and reporting lag. |
| L3 | 订单 / 产能 / 认证 / 客户验证 | Check definitions, units, timing, cancellation terms, yield, and customer status. |
| L4 | 多源交叉验证 | Require independent sources that test different parts of the claim. |
| L5 | 反证仍成立 | State which serious counterarguments were tested and why the hypothesis remains viable. |

## Confidence Rules

- Evidence quality and hypothesis confidence are separate fields.
- Company guidance is primary for what the company said, not automatic proof that the forecast will occur.
- Repeated articles derived from one original source count as one source.
- Missing dates, units, system boundaries, or comparison bases reduce confidence.
- Raise confidence only after seeking evidence that could falsify the claim.
