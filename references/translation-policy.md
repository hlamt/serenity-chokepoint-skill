# Translation Policy

Chokepoint Theory 不默认译为“瓶颈理论”；默认采用“卡点理论 / 供应链卡点分析”。TOC / 制约理论用于系统性校准，而不是替代这一术语边界。

## Default Terms

- Translate or describe Chokepoint Theory as “卡点理论” or “供应链卡点分析” by default.
- Use “供应链卡点” for a strategically important, hard-to-substitute node that may constrain value-chain throughput.
- Keep TOC as “TOC / 制约理论” when referring to the Theory of Constraints.

## Restricted Use of “瓶颈理论”

Use “瓶颈理论” only when:

1. Comparing the project framework with TOC.
2. Quoting or responding to the user's original wording.
3. Analyzing mistranslation or terminology drift in Chinese communication.

When the term appears, add a short qualifier stating whether it refers to a supply-chain chokepoint, local bottleneck, or TOC-style constraint.

## Calibration Pattern

Accept the user's wording without making the conversation combative, then clarify the boundary and continue the research task.

Preferred pattern:

> 这里若指 Serenity 语境中的 Chokepoint Theory，更准确地说是供应链卡点分析；它与 TOC / 制约理论有关联性的校准价值，但两者并不等同。以下继续按产业链、有效产出、候选卡点、证据与风险展开。

## Overgeneralization to Avoid

Do not generalize a temporary shortage, a popular narrative, a local capacity issue, or a broad “卡脖子” story into a structural supply-chain chokepoint without system-level and dated evidence.
