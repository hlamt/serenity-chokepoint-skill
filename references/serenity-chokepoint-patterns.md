# Serenity Chokepoint Patterns

This file is a placeholder for future distillation of publicly available Serenity expressions. Add no claim without a source, date, verbatim-or-paraphrase label, and project interpretation. Do not perform personality simulation.

# Demand Shock

Record evidence that demand changed faster or more persistently than supply planning assumed.

# Supply Scarcity

Record physical, technical, regulatory, geographic, or organizational causes of constrained supply.

# Substitution Difficulty

Record qualification, compatibility, switching-cost, performance, yield, and ecosystem barriers.

# Certification / Qualification Barrier

Record customer validation duration, regulatory certification, reliability testing, and approved-vendor requirements.

# Capacity Expansion Lag

Record construction, tooling, hiring, yield ramp, permitting, financing, and customer-acceptance lead times.

# Customer Lock-in

Record design-in, workflow integration, installed base, data, service, and switching dependencies.

# Pricing Power

Record realized price, mix, gross margin, contract terms, allocation behavior, and customer response.

# Mispricing Signals

Record the gap between dated operating evidence and market expectations without treating valuation disagreement as proof.
