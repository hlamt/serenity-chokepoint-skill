# Risk and Compliance

## Research Risks

| Risk | Research question |
| --- | --- |
| 数据滞后 | Is the supply-demand condition still current as of the analysis date? |
| 公开资料不完整 | Which material assumptions depend on unavailable customer, yield, or contract data? |
| 估值已充分反映 | Is operational scarcity already embedded in expectations or valuation? |
| 制约迁移 | Where does the limiting factor move after expansion or substitution? |
| 替代技术 | What technical or architectural change could bypass the candidate node? |
| 客户压价 | Can customers dual-source, redesign, integrate backward, or use scale to capture value? |
| 政策与监管 | Could export controls, subsidies, antitrust, licensing, or localization alter the thesis? |
| 流动性风险 | Can market depth, volatility, or funding conditions dominate fundamental evidence? |
| 研究假设失效 | Which observable facts would make the original mechanism false? |

## Compliance Rules

- Use public, authorized, traceable information only.
- Do not store cookies, sessions, credentials, brokerage data, portfolios, or private chats.
- Do not imply official Serenity endorsement, TOC certification, or personality simulation.
- Do not present candidate companies as recommendations.
- Include source dates, uncertainty, counterevidence, and invalidation conditions.
- End every research output with the project disclaimer.
