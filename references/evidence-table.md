- `evidence_id`: Evidence identifier, such as `E001`.
- `claim`: The judgment the evidence supports or weakens.
- `source_type`: Filing, financial report, prospectus, news, industry report, order, capacity data, certification information, or another traceable type.
- `source_title`: Source or document title.
- `source_url`: Public link. If unavailable, mark it `TBD` and explain the document locator in `notes`.
- `date`: Source date.
- `evidence_level`: `L0` through `L5` under `references/evidence-ladder.md`.
- `supports`: The claim or sub-claim supported.
- `contradicts`: The claim or sub-claim contradicted or weakened.
- `confidence`: `low`, `medium`, or `high`.
- `notes`: Units, scope, source independence, limitations, and other context.
